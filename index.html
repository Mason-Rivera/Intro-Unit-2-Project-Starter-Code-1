History of The Rickroll

SECTION 1

Rick Astley released this hit single on July 27th 1987 and charted number 1 on March 12th of 1988

SECTION 2

During the early days of the internet this song rose to new heights in popularity as it began being used as a bait and switch meme. This phenomenon became known as "Rickrolling" and it remains popular even today as a timeless classic.

SECTION 3

In recent time even Rick Astley has embraced the unprecidented popularity of his song, often performing it at the musical performances he attends.
